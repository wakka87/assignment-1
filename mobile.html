Static Security

Static security is commonly used to provide a visible and physical presence, deter potential threats, and maintain the safety and security of a particular site. It is often utilized in a variety of settings, including commercial buildings, industrial facilities, residential properties, retail establishments, and event venues, among others. Static security can be an essential component of an overall security strategy to protect assets, people, and property.

Concierge Security

Concierge security is a specialized security service that combines traditional security functions with customer service and hospitality. It is typically associated with upscale residential and commercial properties, hotels, and other environments where providing a high level of service and professionalism is a priority. Concierge security personnel, often referred to as "security concierges," offer security and customer service solutions in a single role.

Event Security

Event security refers to the planning, implementation, and management of security measures and services to ensure the safety and well-being of attendees, participants, and the overall success of an event. Events can vary widely in size and nature, including concerts, sporting events, festivals, conferences, conventions, trade shows, and private gatherings. Event security is crucial to mitigate risks, maintain order, and address potential security threats that may arise during the event.
