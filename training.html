At Knox Security, we hold the conviction that our personnel constitute our greatest asset. This is why we prioritize the training of all our staff to the utmost standards. Our team members undergo meticulous vetting, are approved by Garda, and are licensed by the Private Security Authority, in compliance with the regulations set forth by the Private Security Services Act of 2004.

Basic Training

This course has been developed by the Security Institute of Ireland (SII). The course is run by SII approved trainers and covers the following areas:

  • Role and Responsibilities of the Security Officer
  • Searching
  • Patrolling
  • Access Control
  • Fire Awareness
  • Security and Emergency Systems
  • Health and Safety
  • The Law
  • Emergencies
  • Communications and Reporting
  • Customer Care and Social Skills

Site Specific Training

Prior to beginning a job each security officer receives site specific training to ensure that he can perform all duties required to the highest possible standards.

Depending on the requirements of the assignment, this training can take several forms. For example, a security officer may spend one or several shifts accompanied by an officer experienced at a particular site to familiarise himself with the duties required.

For new positions, our management team will work closely with the customer to ensure that all requirements are understood and documented. Management will then spend time on site with the security officer to familiarise him with the requirements.
