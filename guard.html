Close Protection

Close protection services, often referred to as executive protection or bodyguard services, are specialized security services designed to ensure the safety and security of individuals who may face higher personal or professional risks. These services are typically provided by highly trained and experienced security personnel known as close protection officers or bodyguards. Close protection services can be required by a range of individuals, including high-profile personalities, executives, politicians, celebrities, and other individuals with elevated security concerns.

What We Offer

Executives may seek personal protection anytime; however, some of the most common situations in which they may contract with a private security company include large-scale layoffs, business or personal travel, board or shareholder meetings, work related disputes or special events.

In these situations, our skilled security personnel can provide a wide range of security services for executives which include:

  • Low-profile protective surveillance
  • Body guard or close protection
  • Security drivers
  • Transportation management
  • Security sweeps of meeting spaces
  • Managing confrontations
  • Assessing potential threats
  • Individual or team security units
