Key Holding & Alarm Response

Picture this familiar situation: you're savoring a well-deserved meal at a restaurant or enjoying a weekend getaway when your phone rings. It's the monitoring company, informing you that your home alarm has been triggered. Wouldn't it be nice if someone could handle the situation and let you continue with your plans hassle-free?

Home Security

The process involves providing us with your home keys and alarm codes, relieving you of the responsibility of responding to alarm activations. When your alarm goes off, the monitoring company contacts us instead of you, and a trained security officer promptly addresses the call. We conduct a thorough investigation into the cause of the alarm activation. If everything is in order, we reset the alarm and secure your property without causing any disturbance. A notification card will be left to inform you of our visit and the findings. In case of an issue, our Officer follows your pre-agreed instructions, taking necessary actions. If required, we contact emergency services and remain at the premises until it is properly secured.
